Game Design and Programming

I am a game designer with a keen interest in mechanics design, reward loop structures and ludonarrative dissonance. My experience playing games informed my style of game design, focusing on functionality and efficiency before design. This comes from my background in programming for various languages like Unity, C#, C++, Java, html and css, where functionality is everything. However, my passion for design stems from the variety for of games I have experienced, both the successes and the failures. Learning from industry practices and my own experiences make me a better designer.
